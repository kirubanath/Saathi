Aaj hum baat karenge — pressure ke time pe apni voice kaise control karein, aur nervousness ko kaise handle karein.

Situation imagine karo:
Interviewer unexpected question poochta hai. Aapka mind blank ho jata hai. Voice shaky ho jaati hai.

Yeh normal hai. Lekin control possible hai.

Pehla part — voice modulation under pressure.

Jab nervous hote ho, teen cheezein hoti hain:
Voice fast ho jaati hai
Pitch high ho jata hai
Sentences cut hone lagte hain

Isko kaise fix karein?

Technique 1 — intentional slowing.

Jab question mile, consciously slow bolna start karo.

Example:
Instead of: “Yes I think I can handle it because…”
Say: “Yes… I believe I can handle this.”

Pause automatically confidence create karta hai.

Technique 2 — sentence chunking.

Long sentence mat bolo. Chhote chunks me bolo.

“First… let me think about the situation. Then… I would approach it like this.”

Ab nervousness handling.

Sabse bada myth — nervousness ko remove karna hai. Nahi. Usse channelize karna hai.

Example:
Hands sweating? Use tissue quietly. Ignore mat karo, manage karo.

Breathing:
4 second inhale, 4 second hold, 4 second exhale. Yeh pattern aapko stabilize karta hai.

Ek real scenario:
Candidate A — fast bol raha hai, words mix ho rahe hain.
Candidate B — thoda slow, pauses le raha hai, clear bol raha hai.

Even if Candidate B ko exact answer nahi pata, woh zyada composed lagega.

Voice control ka ek aur trick:
Start strong.

First sentence agar stable hai, baaki flow automatically improve ho jata hai.

Example:
“I haven’t faced this exact situation, but here’s how I would approach it.”

Ab aap control me ho.

Yaad rakho — pressure me perfection nahi chahiye. Stability chahiye.

Agar aap slow, clear aur controlled voice maintain karte ho, toh aapka confidence naturally visible hota hai.

Practice karo — intentionally difficult questions ke saath. Tabhi real improvement aayega.
