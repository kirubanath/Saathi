Aaj hum baat karenge interviews ke ek bahut underrated topic pe — body language, aur kaise nervousness ko handle karein.

Sabse pehle, ek simple situation imagine karo. Aap interview room me enter karte ho. Aapka resume strong hai, lekin jaise hi aap baithte ho, aapke haath hil rahe hain, shoulders slump ho gaye, eye contact break ho raha hai. Ab interviewer kya sochega? Confidence low hai.

Body language ka matlab sirf seedha baithna nahi hai. It’s about signals jo aap bina bole bhejte ho.

Example lete hain.

Galat approach:
Chair pe aise baithna jaise aapko jaldi nikalna hai. Hands lap me chipka ke rakhna. Eye contact avoid karna.

Sahi approach:
Chair ke edge pe nahi, poora support leke baitho. Shoulders relaxed but straight. Hands visible rakho — table pe ya comfortably rested. Jab interviewer bol raha ho, slight nod karo. Yeh show karta hai ki aap engaged ho.

Ab nervousness ki baat karte hain. Nervous feel karna normal hai. Problem tab hoti hai jab woh body language me leak ho jata hai.

Ek trick: controlled breathing.

Interview start hone se pehle 5 deep breaths lo. Slow inhale, slow exhale. Isse heart rate calm hota hai.

Second trick: micro pauses.

Jab question poocha jata hai, turant bolna start mat karo. 2 second rukna perfectly fine hai. Yeh aapko composed dikhata hai.

Third example:
Agar aapka haath thoda shake kar raha hai, use hide mat karo. Instead, pen pakad lo ya hands ko lightly interlock kar lo. Isse movement controlled ho jata hai.

Ek aur important cheez — entry and exit.

Room me enter karte waqt light smile. Exit karte waqt bhi confident posture maintain karo. Bohot log end me relax ho jaate hain aur slouch kar dete hain. That breaks the impression.

Quick recap through example:
Candidate A — strong answers but poor body language, constantly looking down.
Candidate B — average answers but steady eye contact, calm posture.

Zyada impactful kaun lagega? Mostly Candidate B.

Isliye yaad rakho — aap jo bolte ho usse pehle log dekhte kya ho.

Aur nervousness ko eliminate karne ki koshish mat karo. Usse manage karo. Controlled breathing, small pauses, aur stable posture — yeh teen cheezein aapko instantly improve karengi.

Next time jab practice karo, mirror ke saamne sirf answers nahi — apni body bhi observe karo.
