Aaj hum baat karenge — salary negotiation for freshers. Kaise apni baat structure karein, aur pushback ke time calm kaise rahein.

Sabse pehle — answering structure.

Jab aapse poocha jata hai: “What are your salary expectations?”

Galat:
“Jo bhi aap de denge theek hai.”

Ya:
“Mujhe 10 lakh chahiye” — bina context.

Sahi approach — structured ask.

Step 1 — appreciation
Step 2 — market reference
Step 3 — your value
Step 4 — clear range

Example:
“I’m really excited about this role. Based on market standards and my skills, I’m looking at a range of X to Y.”

Ab yeh professional lagta hai.

Ab handling nervousness.

Negotiation me pressure aata hai — especially jab recruiter pushback karta hai.

Example:
“That’s too high.”

Aap panic ho jaate ho, immediately bolte ho — “Okay okay lower kar dete hain.”

Yeh mistake hai.

Instead — pause.

Breath lo. Phir calmly respond karo.

Example:
“I understand. Could you share the budget range for this role?”

Ab conversation balanced ho gayi.

Ek aur scenario:
Recruiter: “We can offer less.”

Aapka structured response:
“Thank you for sharing. I’m flexible, but I’d like to understand if there’s room for adjustment based on performance or joining bonus.”

Notice — calm tone, clear structure.

Nervousness control ke liye:
Slow bolna
Pauses lena
Aur prepared lines rehearse karna

Ek trick:
3–4 sentences ready rakho jo aap confidently bol sako.

Real example:
Candidate A — panic, quickly agrees.
Candidate B — composed, structured, asks questions.

Kaun better deal leke niklega? Obviously Candidate B.

Yaad rakho — negotiation fight nahi hai. It’s a structured conversation.

Agar aap clear ho, calm ho, aur apni baat logically rakhte ho — aapka outcome automatically improve hota hai.
