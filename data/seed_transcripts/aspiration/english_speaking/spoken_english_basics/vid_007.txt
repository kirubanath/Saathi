Aaj hum baat karenge — everyday vocabulary aur fluency. Kaise aap daily conversations me better express kar sakte ho.

Sabse pehle — vocabulary.

Problem kya hoti hai?
Log limited words use karte hain.

Example:
Har cheez ke liye “good” bolna.

Instead:
Good → great, useful, effective, helpful

Sentence example:
“This is a good idea” → “This is a very effective idea”

Ab aapka expression improve ho gaya.

Ek aur example:
“I am very tired”
→ “I’m exhausted”

Vocabulary ka matlab fancy words nahi — right words.

Ab fluency.

Fluency ka matlab hai — bina ruk ruk ke bolna.

Galti:
Word search karte rehna mid sentence.

Example:
“I went to… uh… that place… where…”

Solution:
Simple words use karo jo aapko already aate hain.

“I went to a restaurant yesterday.”

Simple but fluent.

Ek technique — substitution.

Agar exact word yaad nahi aa raha, sentence change karo.

Instead of getting stuck, rephrase.

Practice:
Daily 5 new words sikho. Unhe sentences me use karo.

Example:
Word: “improve”
Sentence: “I want to improve my communication.”

Fluency exercise:
30 seconds topic pe bolo — bina rukhe.

Topic: “My day”

“I woke up early, then I worked on my tasks, then I met my friend…”

Doesn’t have to be perfect — just continuous.

Real comparison:
Speaker A — advanced words but stuck.
Speaker B — simple words but smooth.

Zyada effective kaun? Speaker B.

Final takeaway:
Vocabulary expands your expression. Fluency carries your message.

Dono combine karoge — toh aap natural aur confident speaker banoge.
