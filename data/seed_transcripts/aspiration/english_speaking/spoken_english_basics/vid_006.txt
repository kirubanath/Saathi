Aaj hum focus karenge — pronunciation aur fluency. Kaise aap clear bol sakte ho aur smooth flow maintain kar sakte ho.

Sabse pehle — pronunciation.

Problem kya hoti hai?
Log words galat pronounce karte hain ya unclear bolte hain.

Example:
“Development” ko “devlopment” bolna
“Comfortable” ko “com-for-table” bolna

Sahi approach — word breakdown.

“Development” → de-vel-op-ment

Har syllable clear bolo.

Practice kaise karein?
Slow repetition.

Word ko 3–4 baar clearly bolo. Phir sentence me use karo.

Example:
“I am working on development projects.”

Ab fluency.

Fluency ka matlab fast bolna nahi hai. Smooth bolna hai.

Galat:
Fast bolna, beech me rukna, “uh… um…” use karna

Sahi:
Steady pace, natural pauses

Example:
“I think… this is a good approach… because it solves the core problem.”

Notice — pauses are fine.

Ek aur trick — linking.

Words ko connect karo.

Instead of:
“I am going to apply”
Say:
“I’m gonna apply”

Yeh natural lagta hai.

Practice exercise:
Daily 2 minute aloud reading.

Koi article lo. Read karo. Focus on clarity + smoothness.

Record yourself. Suno — kya words clear hain? Kya flow break ho raha hai?

Ek real comparison:
Speaker A — perfect grammar but unclear pronunciation.
Speaker B — simple sentences but clear and smooth.

Zyada understandable kaun? Speaker B.

Isliye focus clarity pe rakho.

Final tip:
Difficult words ki list banao. Unhe daily practice karo.

Pronunciation improves with repetition. Fluency improves with consistency.

Roz thoda practice karoge — toh noticeable improvement dikhega.
