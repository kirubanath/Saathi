Title: How to speak confidently in interviews
Category: Career & Jobs
Duration: ~8 minutes

---

Namaste, aur welcome back. Aaj hum ek aisi cheez ke baare mein baat karenge jo interview mein bahut bada difference banati hai — lekin zyaadatar log isko kabhi practice nahi karte. Hum aapke resume ki baat nahi kar rahe, na hi qualifications ki. Hum baat kar rahe hain ki aap room mein kaise enter karte ho — aapka body, aapki awaaz, aur aapka mindset — pehle second se lekar last second tak.

Chaliye shuru karte hain body language se, kyunki yahan pe zyaadatar log interview start hone se pehle hi points kho dete hain.

Jab aap interview room mein enter karte ho, interviewer tab se hi ek impression bana raha hota hai. Research kehta hai ki log pehle 30 seconds mein judgment kar lete hain. Toh pehle 30 seconds mein aap apne body ke saath kya karte ho — yeh matter karta hai.

Pehli cheez — posture. Seedha baitho. Stiff nahi, lekin slouch bhi nahi. Samjho ki aap tall baithe ho. Jab aap jhuke hue baithe ho, toh yeh low confidence ka signal deta hai. Jab aap seedha baithe ho, toh yeh readiness aur respect dikhata hai.

Doosri cheez — eye contact. Yeh sabse powerful signal hai jo aap de sakte ho. Jab koi tumse question poochhe, toh unhe dekhte raho jab tak woh bol rahe hain. Jab aap answer do, natural eye contact banao — intense stare nahi, lekin floor bhi mat dekho. Baar baar nazar hatana nervousness ka signal deta hai, chahe tum bilkul honest kyun na ho.

Teesri cheez — haath. Haath table pe ya godi mein rakho. Arms fold mat karo — yeh ek barrier create karta hai. Pen ya phone ke saath fidget mat karo. Still haath calm signal karte hain. Hilte haath anxiety signal karte hain.

Ab baat karte hain awaaz ki.

Voice modulation ka matlab sirf clearly bolna nahi hai. Yeh pace, tone, aur energy ke baare mein hai. Bahut log interviews mein nervous hone ki wajah se bahut fast bolte hain — words rush mein nikal jaate hain aur interviewer follow nahi kar pata. Slow down karo. Thoda slower pace confidence signal karta hai.

Tone bhi matter karta hai. Flat, monotone awaaz mein brilliant answers bhi boring lagte hain. Apni pitch vary karo — jab koi important point ho, thoda rise karo, emphasis ke liye pause karo. Kuch bhi loud padh ke practice karo aur notice karo ki naturally kahan ruko aur kahan speed up karte ho.

Ab baat karte hain questions ka answer dene ke baare mein.

Sabse badi galti jo log karte hain woh hai structure ke bina answer karna. Bolna shuru karte hain, circles mein jaate hain, aur kuch kehne ke bina bahut kuch bol dete hain. Interviewer yeh turant notice karta hai.

Sabse simple structure jo tum use kar sakte ho: pehle situation, phir tumne kya kiya, phir result. Isko STAR method kehte hain — Situation, Task, Action, Result. Lekin naam mein mat ulajhna. Principle simple hai: mujhe batao kya ho raha tha, mujhe batao tumne specifically kya kiya, aur mujhe batao uski wajah se kya hua.

Example ke liye — agar koi poochhe "Tell me about a time you handled pressure" — sirf "I am good under pressure" mat kaho. Kaho: "Meri previous job mein, ek deadline unexpectedly do din pehle aa gayi. Maine list banai ki kya absolutely zaroori hai, team ko communicate kiya ki kya delay ho sakta hai, aur humne time pe deliver kiya. Mere manager ne yeh meri review mein specifically mention kiya." Yeh ek structured answer hai. Yeh yaad rehta hai.

Ab baat karte hain us cheez ki jo koi nahi karta: nervousness.

Har koi nervous hota hai interviews mein. Har koi. Interviewer bhi, jo chahta hai ki conversation achhi tarah se ho. Goal yeh nahi hai ki nervousness khatam karo — yeh possible nahi hai aur zaroori bhi nahi. Goal yeh hai ki nervousness aapke body ya words ko control na kare.

Yeh actually help karta hai: answer dene se pehle slowly breathe karo. Jab question aaye, ek second lo — sirf ek — bolna shuru karne se pehle. Woh pause signal karta hai ki tum soch rahe ho, panic nahi kar rahe. Aur yeh aapke brain ko organize karne ka moment bhi deta hai.

Ek aur cheez jo help karti hai: khud ko yaad dilao ki nervousness aur excitement body mein almost identical feel hote hain. Heart rate up hai, attention sharp hai. Yeh aapka body hai jo tumhe achha karne ke liye prepare kar raha hai. Isko reframe karo. Tum scared nahi ho — tum ready ho.

Toh chaliye recap karte hain. Shoulders back aur chin up karke enter karo. Seedha baitho. Eye contact banao. Ek pace pe bolo jise interviewer follow kar sake. Apne answers structure karo — kya hua, tumne kya kiya, outcome kya tha. Aur jab nervous feel karo, breathe karo, pause karo, aur shuru karo.

Tumne prepare kiya hai. Tum apna kaam jaante ho. Ab apne body aur awaaz ko yeh dikhane do.
